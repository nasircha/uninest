About Uninest

Our mission is to make finding safe and affordable hostels easy for students.

Why Uninest?

Finding a suitable hostel can be stressful and time-consuming for college and university students. Uninest solves this problem by providing a platform where students can:

  • Search for verified hostels and PGs in their city or near their college.
  • View live availability of rooms to avoid unnecessary phone calls.
  • Check hostel facilities including mess options, gender-specific hostels, and safety measures.
  • Submit proof (like Aadhar or PAN) for secure booking when confirming a hostel.
  • See ratings and feedback from other students to make an informed decision.
  • Optionally choose independent mess services even if they are not staying in a hostel.

Our platform ensures students have a safe, transparent, and hassle-free experience finding accommodation and food options during their studies.
